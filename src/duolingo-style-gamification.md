*Gamify your Anki à la Duolingo! Simple visual, audio, and haptic feedback to enrich your studying.*


This add-on is a fixed version for Anki25+ of the addon <a href="https://ankiweb.net/shared/info/255048658" target="_blank">"Duolingo Style Gamification"</a>, originally created by <a href="https://github.com/avanteGarbage" target="_blank">avanteGarbage</a> and credit goes to them. (Not affiliated with the official Duolingo.)






* Updated review buttons
* Popup feedback
* Chimes for incorrect/correct answers
* Controller vibration!
