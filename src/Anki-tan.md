### What is Anki-tan?

Anki-tan is a doodle I drew for [April fool's day this year(2025, reddit)](https://www.reddit.com/r/Anki/comments/1joswls/today_april_1st_is_magical_girl_ankitans_12th/). (Nothing to do with official Anki.) To be frank I was too lazy to draw it so the next appearance was scheduled for April 1 next year, but some Anki users requested me so I added it a bit more. The next appearance is still undecided.

**Design:** It's inspired by [Wikipe-tan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Wikipe-tan) and [Anki's fan art](https://www.reddit.com/r/Anki/comments/gt3zcb/made_an_anki_fanart_to_express_my_gratitude/). The floating flashcards are inspired by the design of [Flandre Scarlet](https://touhou.fandom.com/wiki/Flandre_Scarlet) a character from the Touhou Project (Touhou is a game that influenced Undertale). "-tan" is Japanese slang and is often used in the names of cute anime characters. (e.g. yomitan, binchou-tan)

**Why Ankitan?:** There are already several fan art characters for Anki but their licensing status is unclear. Also if you use them without permission, authors may get angry or restrictions may be placed on how they can be used. By drawing them myself I can avoid all of these issues.
