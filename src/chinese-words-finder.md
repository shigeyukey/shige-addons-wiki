This add-on finds all Chinese words in the [CC-CEDICT dictionary](https://cc-cedict.org/wiki/start) that only use the characters in the decks you select (sorted by frequency), missing HSK words and analyzes the frequency of characters.

![alt text](images/chinese-words-finder/00.png)

This add-on is a fixed version for Anki25+ of the addon <a href="https://ankiweb.net/shared/info/2048169015" target="_blank">"Chinese Words Finder"</a>, originally created by <a href="https://github.com/ThoreBor" target="_blank">Thore Tyborski</a> and credit goes to them.
