##  1. Card status 
The material on the right side glows according to card status.

1. **New card:** Bright blue
1. **Learning:** Blue
1. **Review card:** Green
1. **Relearning:** Red
1. **Relearning+leech:** Purple

## 2. Line thickness
Line thickness can be adjusted in the settings.(0~10) If you set it to 0, it can be hidden.<br>
