[ AnkiArcade ] How to use Wiki

AnkiArcade is an add-on with multiple mini games, progress bar, pomodoro timer, and more.
This is a prototype add-on that I personally developed for the open source flashcard app Anki.
if you become a Patreon, you will get early access to Prototype.(It's not related to the official Anki)
